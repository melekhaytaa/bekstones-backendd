Why Choose Stone Cladding for Garden Walls?

Natural stones are resistant to outdoor weather conditions and maintain their form for many years. Their organic appearance adds warmth and character to your garden.

  • Elegant and natural look
  • Durability against weather conditions like rain, sun, and frost

Which Stones Are Suitable?

Travertine, slate, and basalt are commonly used in garden wall cladding. These stones offer natural texture and a variety of color and pattern options.

Application Tips

You can fully cover your garden wall with stone or highlight specific sections. Combine with plants, lighting, or iron accents to create striking compositions.
