What is Natural Stone Cladding? Where Is It Used?

Natural stone cladding is a decorative application added to exterior or interior surfaces to provide both aesthetics and durability. This age-old technique offers versatile solutions for both traditional and modern architecture.

Why Choose Natural Stone Cladding?

Stone cladding is not only visually appealing but also provides durability, insulation, and a natural touch. On exteriors, it acts as a protective shield for buildings, while in interiors it adds character to the walls.

What Are the Advantages?

Natural stones are highly resistant to heat and harsh weather. They also help with sound and thermal insulation. Over time, they do not fade, crack, or require maintenance.

  • Long-lasting performance
  • Unique and natural patterns
  • Contributes to sound and heat insulation
  • Low maintenance

Where Is It Used?

Natural stone cladding is suitable for many areas:

  • Villa and residential facades
  • Garden walls
  • Interior wall decoration
  • Fireplace surrounds
  • Commercial spaces like restaurants and hotels

Which Stones Are Preferred?

Travertine, andesite, basalt, natural granite, and slate are commonly used materials in stone cladding. Each stone has its own unique texture and color, adding distinctive character to every project.
